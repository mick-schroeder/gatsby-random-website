Have you ever felt overwhelmed by the sheer volume of websites out there? Puzzled about which one to explore next? Fallen into the habit of visiting the same few sites over and over? Don't worry. We are here to help you with our curated selection of links.

## How Does It Work?

With just a click a button, our application redirects you to a random website from our curated list.

But this isn't just about random silly websites or wasting time...

Every website you're directed to is chosen by our editor, curated to ensure you're always in the loop with the most influential websites.

## Why Choose Web Shuffle?

1. **Experience:** An web DJ, seamlessly transitioning you from one influential website to the next.
2. **Top-Tier Sources:** Our algorithm ensures that you never land on mediocre sites. Only the best and most influential make the cut.
3. **Transparency:** Curious about our list of sources? Check them out [here](https://webshuffle.mickschroeder.com/sources).
4. **Alternative to StumbleUpon and The Useless Web:** While they might be fun for casual browsing, our algorithm specifically targets a curated list of premier websites.
5. **Open Source & Free:** Our code is available free for everyone on [GitHub](https://github.com/mick-schroeder/schroeder-Web Shuffle).
